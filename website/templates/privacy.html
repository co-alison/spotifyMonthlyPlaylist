Privacy

Here's what you need to know:

  • Music Lane uses data from your Spotify account, including your saved tracks, top tracks, and followed artists to generate personalized playlists for you.
  • Your personal information is not stored or shared anywhere, including your email address or password.
  • Your data is only used to create playlists and is not used for any other purpose.
  • If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, please don't hesitate to contact me.

If you wish to revoke Music Lane's permissions, you can visit your apps page and click "REMOVE ACCESS" on Music Lane. Here is more detailed instructions on how to do so.
