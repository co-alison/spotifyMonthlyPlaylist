About

Welcome to Music Lane! My website creates custom playlists based on your listening history and preferences. With just a few clicks, you can travel back in time by generating a playlist featuring your favourite tracks, new releases from your go-to artists in that era, plus recomendations based on your listening habits.

Music Lane utilizes Spotify's Web API to access your account data, including your saved tracks, top tracks, and followed artists. Click here to learn more about our privacy policy.

To get started, select the month and year you want to create a playlist for, and we'll do the rest. For best results, select a month and year when you saved a lot of tracks. Note: your top tracks are only included if you select the last month and year.

I hope you enjoy your music time capsules!
